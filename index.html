The Manifesto

OUR IDEOLOGY

Seven principles. No asterisks. No fine print. No "conditions apply." The scorpion's venom is honest — it doesn't discriminate.

01
Zero Corruption Tolerance

No excuses. No "coalition compulsion." Any leader caught in corruption is removed and punished. The scorpion doesn't negotiate with the infection — it removes it.

02
Equal Accountability

No VIP culture. No "senior neta" discount. Every party, every leader, every godman — questioned equally. The stinger doesn't check your party card before striking.

03
Youth First

No paper leaks. No exam delays. No fake promises with folded hands at rallies. Students' time is not a campaign prop. Respect it or face the sting.

04
Development Over Drama

Infrastructure, healthcare, jobs — these are not "boring." Political fights, manufactured controversies, and prime-time screaming matches are. We choose boring.

05
Civic Sense as Education

Traffic rules. Law awareness. Public responsibility. Teach it in schools. A nation that doesn't respect shared spaces can't expect shared progress.

06
Fast & Strong Justice

Time-bound courts. Death penalty for crimes involving children — no loopholes, no bail tourism. Justice delayed is justice sold to the highest bidder.

07
No Trend Politics

Serious national issues are not for clout, memes, or 48-hour hype cycles. If you have no solution and no action — you have no platform. Silence is better than noise.

"We do not promise Ram Mandir 2.0, a bharat jodo yatra season 3, or another PIL about a meme. We promise to be the party that actually reads the bills before voting on them. A radical concept in Indian politics. The scorpion doesn't hype. It stings."
